# MD7 Sales Academy
## Information Architecture

---

# Purpose

This document defines how every piece of information inside the platform should be organized.

The objective is to create an application where knowledge is discoverable through multiple paths rather than requiring users to read chapters in order.

A learner should be able to begin with:

• A chapter

• An objection

• A customer profile

• An engagement stage

• A framework

• A conversation

• A glossary term

and naturally discover related knowledge.
